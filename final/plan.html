WDD 231: Final Project Plan

Site Name

Flake Family

This name reflects the focus of the site, which is centered around my immediate family. It creates a personal and welcoming space for sharing memories, showcasing family members, and preserving stories.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Flake Family website is to preserve and share memories, stories, and profiles of our immediate family. The site includes a dynamic homepage with family member profiles, a post section with meaningful memories and photos, and a form for visitors to contribute their own family stories or messages.

Scenarios

How can I learn more about each member of the Flake family?

Where can I read or share special family memories and stories?
